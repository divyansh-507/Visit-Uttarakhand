About Us

Hello! Welcome to Visit Uttarakhand. Hope you have a wonderful time ...

Visit Uttarakhand is a website that serves you as a complete tourist guide, for the people who want to explore this beutiful state in the north. Uttarakhand is referred to as "Devbhoomi", which means 'The land of the GODS', and has a lot of history which is still unexplored by the people of India or outside. People only the famous places like Kedarnath, Auli which is a winter destination of tourists, Mussoorie which is a beautiful hill station near Dehradun. So, through this website we aim to provide people the chance to Explore the Unexplored and get to know the other hidden places of this beautiful state and get to know its history. Also Uttarakhand is a diverse state and it's culture is unique in it's own way! So you can get to know about it's diversity & culture as well and a list of adventures you can do while visiting this region in the north. Hope you have a wonderful journey !!
